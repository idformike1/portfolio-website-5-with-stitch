My Story

A former competitive sprinter, Aisha understands the fine margins between winning and losing. Her journey from the track to the lab was fueled by a singular question: How can we optimize the human engine through what we consume?

This curiosity led her to an MSc in Sport and Exercise Nutrition from the prestigious Loughborough University, world-renowned for its athletic research. There, she honed her ability to translate complex biochemical data into actionable dietary strategies.
