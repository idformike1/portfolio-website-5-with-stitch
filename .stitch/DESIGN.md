# Design System: Aisha Rao Portfolio

Visual identity and design tokens for the Aisha Rao brand.

## 1. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Space Grotesk (Google Fonts)
- **Scale:**
  - `oversized-heading`: `clamp(4rem, 15vw, 12rem)`, bold, uppercase, tracking `-0.05em`.
  - `h2`: `text-6xl md:text-8xl`, tracking-tighter.
  - `body`: `text-base` or `text-lg`.
  - `caps-label`: `text-[10px]`, font-bold, uppercase, tracking `widest`.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary:** `#6B705C` (Olive/Sage Green)
- **Primary Dark:** `#4A4E3D`
- **Background (Main):** `#F0F0E8` (Beige/Off-white)
- **Foreground (Text):** `#333333` (Slate Custom)
- **Accent:** Colors from images are often grayscale or muted.

## 3. Layout Patterns
- **Max Width:** `1440px` (Main Container)
- **Gutters:** `px-6 md:px-12`
- **Section Spacing:** `py-24 md:py-32`
- **Dividers:** `1px solid #d1d1ca`

## 4. Components Style
- **Cards:** Rounded `xl` (0.75rem), often with subtle borders or shadow.
- **Buttons:** Bold uppercase text, minimal icons (North East arrow).
- **Navigation:** Top-bar style, font-bold, uppercase, tracking-widest.

## 5. Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
When generating new pages for this project, always adhere to the following:
- Use **Space Grotesk** as the only font.
- Maintain the **Olive Green (#6B705C)** and **Beige (#F0F0E8)** color scheme.
- Use **oversized headings** for section titles.
- Keep the aesthetic **minimalist, high-end, and editorial**.
- Use **grayscale images** or images with high contrast and low saturation.
- Include **Science-backed** or **Performance** oriented micro-copy.
